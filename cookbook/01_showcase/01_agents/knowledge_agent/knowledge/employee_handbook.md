# Employee Handbook

## Welcome to Acme Corp

Welcome to the team! This handbook outlines our policies, benefits, and expectations for all employees.

## Time Off and Leave

### Paid Time Off (PTO)

All full-time employees receive:
- **First year**: 15 days PTO
- **Years 2-5**: 20 days PTO
- **Years 5+**: 25 days PTO

PTO can be used for vacation, personal days, or sick leave. Unused PTO does not roll over to the next year and cannot be cashed out.

### Requesting PTO

1. Submit requests through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance for planned time off
2. For sick days, notify your manager by 9 AM on the day of absence
3. Requests over 5 consecutive days require manager and HR approval

### Holidays

Acme Corp observes the following paid holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (2 days)
- Christmas Eve and Christmas Day
- New Year's Eve

### Parental Leave

- Primary caregiver: 16 weeks paid leave
- Secondary caregiver: 8 weeks paid leave
- Leave must be taken within 12 months of birth or adoption

## Benefits

### Health Insurance

We offer three health plan options:
1. **HMO Plan**: Low premiums, requires in-network providers
2. **PPO Plan**: Higher premiums, more flexibility in provider choice
3. **High-Deductible Plan**: Lowest premiums, HSA eligible

Open enrollment occurs annually in November. New hires can enroll within 30 days of start date.

### 401(k) Retirement Plan

- Company matches 50% of contributions up to 6% of salary
- Immediate vesting of company match
- Roth and traditional options available

### Other Benefits

- Life insurance (2x annual salary)
- Short and long-term disability
- Employee assistance program (EAP)
- Gym membership reimbursement ($50/month)
- Professional development budget ($1,500/year)

## Work Policies

### Remote Work

Acme Corp supports flexible work arrangements:
- **Hybrid**: Default for most roles (3 days office, 2 days remote)
- **Fully Remote**: Available for certain positions with manager approval
- **Office-Based**: Required for some roles due to job requirements

### Work Hours

Standard work hours are 9 AM to 5 PM local time. Flexible schedules may be arranged with manager approval. All employees are expected to be available for core hours (10 AM - 3 PM) for meetings and collaboration.

### Expense Reimbursement

Employees may be reimbursed for approved business expenses:
- Submit receipts within 30 days of expense
- Approval required for expenses over $100
- Use company credit card for travel bookings

## Code of Conduct

### Professional Standards

All employees are expected to:
- Treat colleagues with respect and dignity
- Maintain confidentiality of company information
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Report concerns through appropriate channels

### Communication

- Use professional language in all work communications
- Respond to emails within 24 business hours
- Use Slack for quick questions, email for formal communications
- Document important decisions in writing

### Anti-Harassment Policy

Acme Corp has zero tolerance for harassment of any kind. Report concerns to HR or use the anonymous ethics hotline.

## Performance and Growth

### Performance Reviews

- Annual reviews conducted in January
- Mid-year check-ins in July
- Continuous feedback encouraged throughout the year

### Promotion Process

1. Discuss goals with your manager
2. Document accomplishments and impact
3. Manager nominates for promotion during review cycle
4. Calibration with leadership team
5. Decisions communicated by end of Q1

## Questions?

Contact HR at hr@acmecorp.example.com or visit the HR portal for additional resources.
